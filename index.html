About NTUMA

About Us

NTUMA is a Kigali-based errand and delivery services helping busy individuals and businesses save time. We handle deliveries, errands, and small tasks through verified agents, with a focus on trust, speed, and accountability.

Mission

NTUMA's mission is to help professionals, full-time employees, and business owners save valuable time by handling their daily errands, deliveries, and personal tasks with reliability and care. We exist to remove distractions caused by time-consuming responsibilities, allowing our clients to focus on their work, increase productivity, and accelerate personal and business growth. By providing trusted assistance through verified agents, Ntuma supports a more efficient, balanced, and forward-moving lifestyle in Rwanda and beyond.

Vision

Our vision is to become the most trusted errand and assistance platform in Rwanda, empowering people to use their time more effectively. We aim to support professionals, employees, and business owners by making everyday tasks easier to delegate, helping them stay focused, productive, and growth-driven. Through reliable service and smart use of technology, Ntuma envisions a future where time is no longer a barrier to progress and development.
